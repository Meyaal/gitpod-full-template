About Us:

Take care of your mind, body and soul. A personal yoga studio in the center of Stockhom that offers Vinyasa flow, Soft yoga flow and Yin yoga. Small classes for 3-6 people and more focus on you.
Warm welcome!

Chose a class:

Vinyasa Flow

Vinyasa Flow is a dynamic class where breathing is synchronized with movement. A warm-up with a focus on breathing to calm the mind and let in focus here and now, to then move on to sequences of strengthening movements and positions that run into each other. Then we go into a slowdown of sitting and lying positions. The class ends with a nice relaxation and the opportunity to find peace in body and soul with active rest.

Soft Yoga Flow

Soft yoga flow Is a slower and softer dynamic class that always begins with a breathing exercise or meditation followed by a gentle flow of positions to land in the body. The class ends with a long savasana. The class is suitable for people who want to take it easy and beginners.

Yin Yoga

Yinyoga is a passive and meditative yoga form with asanas (positions) that is held for a longer time of about 3-5 minutes, sometimes even longer. The positions are mostly performed sitting or lying on the floor. Yinyoga is de-stressing and a great way to get to know your body.
